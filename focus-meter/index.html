Focus-Meter

How to use:

-Start working
-Gain a focus-point every ten minutes
-Whenever get distracted, subtract a focus-point
-When number of focus-points reaches zero, take a break
-Max focus-points = 3
-Mechanism of work: you don't want to lose, so you keep focused

Based on Codecademy's counter.
